Frequently Asked Questions

How does the internship matching algorithm work?

Our AI-powered matching algorithm analyzes your profile, including your skills, academic background, interests, and career goals. It then compares this information with the requirements and preferences of internship opportunities in our database to find the best matches for you.

The algorithm also takes into account factors such as location preferences, stipend expectations, and availability to provide personalized recommendations that align with your specific needs and aspirations.

How can I improve my match percentage?

To improve your match percentage, make sure your profile is complete and up-to-date. Here are some specific tips:

  • Add all relevant skills, especially those in demand for your desired roles
  • Upload a comprehensive resume with your latest experience and projects
  • Complete your education details, including relevant coursework
  • Add any certifications or additional qualifications you have
  • Specify your internship preferences clearly (location, duration, industry, etc.)
  • Keep your profile regularly updated with new skills and experiences

What should I do if I'm not getting any matches?

If you're not receiving any matches, consider the following steps:

  • Review and update your skills to ensure they match current industry demands
  • Broaden your location preferences or consider remote opportunities
  • Adjust your stipend expectations if they might be too high for the market
  • Check if your availability aligns with typical internship periods
  • Consider adding more industries or roles to your preferences
  • Contact our support team for personalized assistance with your profile

How do I track my internship applications?

You can track all your internship applications through your dashboard or the Applications page. For each application, you'll be able to see:

  • Current status (Submitted, Under Review, Interview Scheduled, etc.)
  • Application submission date
  • Any updates or communications from the employer
  • Upcoming interview details (if applicable)
  • Action items or next steps

You'll also receive email notifications whenever there's an update to your application status.

Can companies see my profile before I apply?

By default, your full profile is only visible to companies after you apply to their internship positions. However, you can adjust your privacy settings to allow companies to discover your profile if they're looking for candidates with your skills and qualifications.

If you enable profile discovery, companies will be able to see your basic information, skills, and qualifications, but your contact details will remain private until you accept their invitation to apply or connect.

You can manage these settings in your Profile Privacy section under Settings.

Is InternMatch only for HIT students?

Currently, InternMatch is exclusively available to students and recent graduates of Harare Institute of Technology. This allows us to provide specialized support and build strong relationships with companies that specifically want to hire HIT talent.

To verify your HIT student status, you need to sign up with your official HIT email address (@hit.ac.zw) and provide your student ID number during registration.

We may expand to other institutions in the future, but our current focus is on serving the HIT community with the best possible internship matching experience.
